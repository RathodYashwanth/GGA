Welcome to GGA
GLOBAL GORS ASSOCIATION (GGA) is to provide a platform for all the like-minded, passionate and committed people who understand and share the culture, heritage and values of Gor community, to participate in its socio-economic development. Preserve, maintain, and perpetuate the cultural heritage of Gor community in US and abroad. Collaborate with other organizations across the globe to promote the cultural, literacy, educational and charitable interests of Gor community. Foster friendship and understanding among Gor community. Raise, solicit, collect, and disburse funds, charities, and donations for cultural, educational, and charitable purposes either directly or in cooperation with other not-for-profit organizations in the US and abroad.

President's Message
GGA strives to be an extended family to all its members. Language binds us as one family across state, religion and national boundaries. GGA will strive to build upon this binding agent to promote the brotherhood among Gor families and will work to contribute back to our less fortunate brothers in sub-continent. Every member of GGA should feel valued and strive to be a productive member of their respective communities. GGA will continue to provide necessary tools, know-how and will inculcate values to our next generation. Let us cherish our heritage and unique festivals that create harmony with the nature.
- Kalidas Porika
